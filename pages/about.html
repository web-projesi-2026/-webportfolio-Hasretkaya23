About Us

What is StudyUp?

StudyUp is a free productivity platform built for students. We combine task management, goal tracking and daily motivation in one clean interface — so you can focus on learning, not juggling tools.

Our Mission

To help every student plan their studies effectively, stay motivated and achieve their academic goals — regardless of where they are or what tools they have.

Our Vision

A world where no student feels overwhelmed by their workload. We believe that the right structure and the right mindset can turn any goal into reality.

Our Values

Simplicity, student-first design and continuous improvement. We ship features that solve real problems — no bloat, no noise, just tools that work.

Our Story

How it started

StudyUp started as a university Web Design course project. The idea was simple: build something students actually need. After countless hours of planning, coding and refining, it became a full-featured platform with task management, goal tracking, a study guide and daily motivation.

We built it with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP — no frameworks, no shortcuts. Every line of code was written to solve a real student problem.

Whether you have an exam tomorrow or a semester-long project ahead, StudyUp is here to help you structure your time, track your goals and keep going.
