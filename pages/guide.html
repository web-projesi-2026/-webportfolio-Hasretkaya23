📖 Study Tips

Work smarter,
learn more.

Science-backed study techniques to boost your learning.

01

Pomodoro Technique

Focus for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break. After 4 pomodoros take a long break.

⏱️ Time Management
02

Active Recall

Close what you read and summarize from memory. Much more effective than passive reading.

🧠 Memory
03

Spaced Repetition

Review topics on day 1, 3, 7 and 30. Transfer knowledge to long-term memory.

📅 Planning
04

Feynman Method

Explain what you learned in simple terms as if teaching someone. Find your gaps.

💡 Understanding
05

Goal-Focused Study

Before each study session answer: "What will I learn in this session?"

🎯 Goals
06

Time Blocking

Divide the day into 2-3 hour blocks. Assign one subject or topic per block.

⏰ Efficiency
